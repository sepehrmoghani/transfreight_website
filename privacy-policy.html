Privacy Policy

Transfreight Privacy Policy
Transfreight Solutions Australia Pty Ltd ABN 36 144 812 537 provides this statement as part of its commitment to user privacy. It details how Transfreight collects and uses personal information within its business and how Transfreight customers can access their own personal information.

The Policy of Transfreight is to comply fully with the Australian Privacy principles for the fair handling of personal information as set out in the Privacy Act 1988 (Cwth) (“Privacy Act”).

Personal Information
Examples of customer information that Transfreight collects includes names, information on shipments, address, email address, contact personnel, ABN details and telephone numbers. Some of the information Transfreight collects is data which relates to an identifiable person.

Collection Purpose
Transfreight uses information about its customers and their transport activity to provide or enhance the services it makes available to customers, to communicate with customers about additional services they may find of value and to assist Transfreight’s legitimate business interests. For example, the setting of credit arrangements and recording payments. Transfreight may also use information for compliance with Government regulation applicable to the transport and freight brokerage industry.

Retention
Any personal information Transfreight collects will be used solely for the purpose of providing its services or as otherwise described in this statement and it will not store it for any longer period than is legally permissible and required for this purpose.

Disclosure
The utilisation of personal data and collection of personal data from customers will be used primarily for the purposes set out above. Transfreight may also disclose such data to a credit reporting agency pursuant to Part 111A of the Privacy Act, in relation to any application to Transfreight for finance or credit.

Except to the extent set out above Transfreight does not share, sell, transfer or otherwise disseminate personal information unless required by law or required for the purpose of an agreement with a particular customer or unless a customer has given express consent so to do.

Access to Data
Customers can ascertain the particular information being held by contacting us through this website. Transfreight will acknowledge your request within three (3) weeks. If Transfreight is prevented from disclosing personal information we will give reasons for such refusal. To keep personal information accurate, please contact Transfreight in respect of any incorrect information so that our records can be updated.

Acknowledgement of Customers Consent
Customers consent to Transfreight using customers personal information for the purposes, uses and disclosures described in this statement.
